Cyber Attacks and AI

Attackers target businesses and companies because they have more holes in their security which makes them easy to attack. The most targeted industries are the energy, financial, and technology sectors. It can cause long and short-term effects. It can shut down operations for hours or even days. Businesses can lose thousands of dollars. It can even cause businesses to lose their reputation.
Some ways to stay safe online are:

Using different passwords for different websites backing up your data
turn on 2-step verification
keep software and operating systems updated
