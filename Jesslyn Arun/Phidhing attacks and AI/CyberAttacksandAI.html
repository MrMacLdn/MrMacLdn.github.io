Cyber Attacks and AI

AI plays an important role in cybersecurity in a both bad and good way. AI is used to protect our devices from threats. Cyber criminals can also use it to attack. AI does time consuming tasks that humans have to do manually. It can scan and find threats. AI can be used to malicios activities like stealing data.
