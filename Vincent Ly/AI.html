  • What is AI in healthcare?

    In the simplest sense, AI is when computers and other machines mimic human cognition, and are capable of learning, thinking, and making decisions or taking actions.

  • How It Works

    AI provide many ways to gather data, analzye it, and provide accurate data-insights. AI is also capable of providing diagnosis more efficiently, It helps professionals provide better treatment and cure diseases.

  • Real-World Examples and Applications

    AI is used in healthcare all around the world as it is a dependent device that professionals use on a daily basis. Some examples include CT scans, X-rays, MRIs and much more.

  • Potential Benefits and Drawbacks

    AI in healthcare, although very affective and efficent, can cause drawbacks for actual professionals. Benefits of AI are less cases of human error, assists medical professionals, and providing patient services 24/7. Some of the drawsbacks of AI in healthcare, can reduce the amount of medical professionals that are needed in hospitals, but with more AI replacing jobs it makes it harder for medical professionals to get employed.

  • Ethical Consideration of AI in Healthcare

    Ethical consideration is an important role in AI, without ethical consideration AI can go rogue. Some of the considerations that are used are informed to consent to data, safety and transparency, algorithmic fairness and biases, and data privacy.
