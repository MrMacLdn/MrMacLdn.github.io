How Phising Attacks can be harmful to people and organizations

Phishing Attacks really disrupt an organization's operations. Once an attacker has found their way arond the network, they can install malware or ransomware which can lead to system outages and other disruptions to your network. In 2020 there was a survey which identified phising attacks as the most harmful cyber atttack in the UK, which isn't much of a suprise, if you know how distributive they can be to an organization. After a phishing attack it takes around 24 hours to restore all the information and assets an organization has lost, which stops work, and the organization loses money. For individuals who have come to be a victim of a phishing attack, after the fact they may feel very helpless and taken advantage of. This can lead to anxiety, stress, and other emotional disruptions.
