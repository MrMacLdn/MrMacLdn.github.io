The potential for AI in Health Care

There is a lot of potential for AI in Health care and the medicine feild. AI in health care can help make things more affordable and efficient. There are many feilds in health care that AI can change and make better.

Ai is already able to detect desease in people 30% faster and is

Money

Training

Ai can greatly help in the field of training medical professionals.

Diagnosing

AI can help in the field of diagnosing patients as well. AI can
read through documents documenting symptoms and can use this
information to diagnose a patient. This can greatly change the
online diagnosing field, if utilized it could greatly help people

Staying Healthy

AI can help people stay healthy by giving good medical advice

help them to help their patients better and more effectively. AI
could easily personalized healthcare making healthcare unique
for every person.

Helping the elderly

Ai can also help with the elderly as they are beginning to die.
