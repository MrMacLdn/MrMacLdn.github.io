What are NLP and ChatBots?
NLP stands for Natural Language Processing; NLP makes AI able to understand human language and abbreviations. This has made it possible for computers to understand us and what we are communicating with it. NLP uses mathematical algorithms to process and study human language data. NLP tools can transform text into something that a computer can understand. Chatbots are an application that everyone can get, you can input text and it will give you an answer to the information that it has access to. It uses NLP to understand what we are saying and then uses NLP to give us an answer in our language.
There are very many examples of NLP, it is used for Chatbots like chat GPT, it is used in things like PowerPoint, word, google docs for speech recognition for speech to text. It is used as an email filter to filter out spam and possible malicious emails. It is also used for language translation for something like google translate. Some real-life examples of Chatbots are used for work, like possible fixing errors in code or summarizing text to help people understand it better.
Some potential benefits to NLP are to further computers' understanding of us and how we speak our language. It also can take very large amounts of text at once and understand what it is about and what it means.
