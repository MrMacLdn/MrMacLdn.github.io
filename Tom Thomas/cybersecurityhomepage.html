Why is Cyber Security Important?

Cyber Security is important because it protects all types of data from theft or loss, including sensitive data, protected health information, protected identifiable information, personal information, business information.



What are some risks and consequnces of cyber attacks?

Cyber attacks can have a lot of risks and consequences, such as loss of productivity, loss of revenue, and loss of reputation. Cyber attacks also have negative effects on society such as inflation, and shortage of products or services.


How to stay cyber safe?

Despite how bad cyber attacks are, there are ways to prevent them such as using stronger passwords, turning on 2-step authentication, thinking before clicking on an unsure link, avoiding public Wi-Fi, and always making sure your software is updated.
